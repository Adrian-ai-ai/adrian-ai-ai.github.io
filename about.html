About us

Karis Foundation was founded in January 2021 aas a non-profit organisation devoted to the development of children and adolescent girls through education. Our foundation seeks to enable them to become leaders actively participating in shaping their world by reducing poverty and pioneering social and economic development. Vision

Our vision is to contribute towards the reduction of poverty, inequality and vulnerability in Zambia.

Mission/Purpose

To empower children and adolescent girls in underprivileged communities to acquire quality education, knowledge and soft skills that will support human and social development in Zambia.

Motto

Blooming excellence through quality education.
